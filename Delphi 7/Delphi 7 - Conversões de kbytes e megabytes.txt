Kilobyte to Megabyte Conversion Example

Task: Convert 3,000 Kilobytes to Megabytes (show work)
Formula:
Kilobytes ÷ 1,024 = Megabytes
Calculations:
3,000 Kilobytes ÷ 1,024 = 2.9296875 Megabytes
Result:
3,000 Kilobytes is equal to 2.9296875 Megabytes
Megabyte to Kilobyte Conversion Example

Task: Convert 25 Megabytes to Kilobytes (show work)
Formula:
Megabytes x 1,024 = Kilobytes
Calculations:
25 Megabytes x 1,024 = 25,600 Kilobytes
Result:
25 Megabytes is equal to 25,600 Kilobytes
